Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Gila Valley Rentals collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our website or submit an application.

Information we collect

  • Information you provide: We will collect your name, email address, mailing address, and mobile phone number when you sign up for SMS updates. The information will be collected via the website contact form, email, rental agreement, or third-party reservation systems
  • Usage information: We use your data solely for informational messages.

How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries and communicate with you.
  • To process and manage rental applications.

Data Security

  • We protect your data with secure storage measures to prevent unauthorized access.

Data Retention

  • We retain your information as long as you are subscribed to our SMS service. You may request deletion at any time.

Sharing of information

We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. Gila Valley Rentals LLC will not sell, rent, or share the collected mobile numbers.

Third-party links

Our website may link to third-party sites (for example, the application portal). Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

OPT-Out

You can opt out of the SMS list at any time by texting, emailing, or replying STOP or CANCEL to Info@gilavalleyrentals.com or (928) 792-1810, (928) 348-2399. After unsubscribing, you will receive a final SMS to confirm you have unsubscribed and we will remove your number from our list within 24 hours.

You can send HELP for additional assistance, and you will receive a text including our Phone number, email and website. We are here to help you.
